FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a cleaning service?

Booking is easy! Fill out our contact form, call or message directly us on whataspp +91 7828597277. Simply choose your service, select a convenient time, and we'll handle the rest!

What areas do you service?

We currently provide professional cleaning services across Ahmedabad, Betul, Chennai, Indore, and Ghaziabad. We are expanding rapidly to other cities soon!

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?

Absolutely. We use 100% eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning agents that are biodegradable and completely safe for your little ones and furry friends.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

It is entirely up to you. Many of our clients provide us with a key or entry code. All our cleaners are thoroughly vetted and background-checked for your peace of mind.

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. However, if you have specific products you prefer us to use, just let us know and we'll accommodate your request.

How long does a typical cleaning take?

Cleaning duration depends on home size and service type. A standard 2-bedroom apartment takes 2-3 hours, while a deep clean for a larger home may take 4-6 hours. We'll provide an estimate when you book.

Is your service guranteed?

Yes! We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with any area we cleaned, let us know within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean it at no charge.
